Resilient

To withstand hardship

  • Focus on the bigger picture and your long-term goals
  • Seek support from friends, family, or professionals
    when needed
  • Practice self-care and engage in activities
    that promote mental and emotional wellbeing
  • Cultivate resilience and develop
    coping mechanisms to manage stress and adversity
  • Remember that hardships are temporary
    and that you are capable of overcoming them

To remain calm and strong
  • Practice mindfulness and focus on the present moment
  • Use deep breathing techniques or meditation to reduce stress and anxiety
  • Visualize a positive outcome and focus on solutions rather than problems
  • Seek support from others when needed, but also develop self-reliance
  • Stay positive and maintain a sense of humor to help maintain perspective.
