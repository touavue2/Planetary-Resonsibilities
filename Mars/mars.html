Empathy

To show hospitality to people

  • Welcome others warmly and make them
    feel comfortable in your space
  • Offer food, drinks, or other amenities to guests
  • Show interest in others by asking questions and
    actively listening to their responses
  • Provide helpful information or guidance to those
    who may be unfamiliar with your area or customs
  • Follow up with guests after their visit
    to show your appreciation for their time and company.

To show forgiveness
  • Let go of grudges and resentment towards others
  • Recognize that everyone makes mistakes and deserves a second chance
  • Take responsibility for your own role in the situation and apologize if necessary
  • Communicate clearly and openly with the other person to resolve the issue
  • Focus on moving forward and rebuilding trust and respect in the relationship
